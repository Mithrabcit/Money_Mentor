# 🚀 AI Money Mentor

### AI-Powered Personal Finance Intelligence Platform for India 🇮🇳

AI Money Mentor is a comprehensive financial planning and portfolio intelligence platform designed to help individuals make data-driven financial decisions through advanced analytics, goal-based planning, and AI-powered insights.

The platform transforms fragmented financial information into a unified financial intelligence system, enabling users to assess their financial health, optimize investments, and build a clear roadmap toward long-term wealth creation and Financial Independence (FIRE).

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Financial planning remains highly fragmented for most individuals.

Users often struggle with:

* Lack of visibility into overall financial health
* Inadequate emergency fund and insurance planning
* Unstructured investment strategies
* Portfolio overlap and asset concentration risks
* Absence of goal-oriented wealth planning
* Difficulty tracking progress toward Financial Independence

Existing solutions typically solve only one aspect of personal finance, forcing users to rely on multiple disconnected platforms.

---

# 🧠 Solution Architecture

AI Money Mentor consolidates financial assessment, planning, and portfolio optimization into a single AI-driven ecosystem.

The platform follows a three-stage financial intelligence workflow:

```text
Financial Assessment
          ↓
Goal & FIRE Planning
          ↓
Portfolio Optimization
```

This approach provides users with actionable insights rather than isolated financial metrics.

---

# ⚡ Core Modules

## 1️⃣ Money Health Score Engine

A comprehensive financial wellness assessment framework that evaluates a user's overall financial stability.

### Key Metrics Analyzed

* Emergency Fund Adequacy
* Insurance Coverage Sufficiency
* Debt-to-Income Ratio
* Savings Rate Efficiency
* Asset Allocation Balance
* Retirement Preparedness
* Liquidity Analysis

### AI-Powered Outputs

* Personalized financial health score
* Risk assessment indicators
* Financial weakness detection
* Actionable improvement recommendations
* Future financial resilience insights

### Example Insights

```text
Emergency fund covers only 2 months of expenses.
Recommended minimum: 6 months.

Debt servicing ratio exceeds healthy threshold.
Consider debt reduction strategy.
```

## 2️⃣ FIRE Path Planner

A goal-based wealth projection engine designed around the Financial Independence Retire Early (FIRE) methodology.

### User Inputs

* Monthly Income
* Fixed Expenses
* Variable Expenses
* Existing Investments
* Desired Retirement Age
* Target Corpus
* Expected Inflation Rate
* Expected Portfolio Return

### Advanced Calculations

* Financial Independence Number
* Safe Withdrawal Rate Analysis
* Monthly SIP Requirement
* Goal Achievement Projection
* Future Corpus Simulation
* Inflation-Adjusted Planning

### Output

* Personalized FIRE Roadmap
* Investment Milestones
* Retirement Timeline Forecast
* Monthly Investment Targets
